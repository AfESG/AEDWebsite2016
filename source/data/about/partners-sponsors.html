Partners & Sponsors

The AfESG and the authors of the African Elephant Status Report 2016 are grateful to our data providers, including AfESG members, range state wildlife agencies, Vulcan Inc's Great Elephant Census, NGOs, individual researchers and interested observers around the African continent. The provision of up-to-date information helps to ensure that the most recent available data are included in the African Elephant Database. Particular thanks are due to Solertium for driving the back-end of the database to help deliver the numbers and tables; Refleqt for their efforts on updating the maps, especially the extensive new range changes; and Flint for the design and delivery of this website and the published and digital versions of the AESR 2016.

Lastly, we thank our primary partners, Vulcan Inc and Save the Elephants, for their support throughout the further development of the AED and the production of the AESR 2016.
