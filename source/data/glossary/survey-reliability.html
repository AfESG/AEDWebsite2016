Survey Reliabilty

The AED incorporates a system to accommodate all types of numbers by classifying them according to their type and designating them as estimates and guesses. The new ADD system uses a scale of survey reliability, ranging from A (highest) to E (lowest). Survey reliability gives an indication of the level of certainty that can be placed on a given number, as determined by the method employed and how it was carried out.
